Why it exists

The ledger does the talking.

A Build Sprint should never feel like a good call that disappears. The ledger makes the value visible after every session and gives the member one place to find their context, agents, workflows, and next steps.

1

Show progress

Every call records what was built, what changed, and what the member can reuse.

2

Reduce confusion

Open loops, access needs, next call focus, and action items live in one place.

3

Earn renewal

The renewal proof section summarizes assets created, time saved, and the next useful build.

Reusable structure

Seven sections. One job each.

Keep the template tight. Each surface should answer a specific member question instead of becoming a random notes dump.
