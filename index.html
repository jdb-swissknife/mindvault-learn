FAQ

A few quick answers before you jump in.

The goal is a personal agent system that is practical, teachable, and able to improve with you over time.

What do you mean by a virtual computer?

A virtual computer is an agent that can work across digital surfaces for you. Browser tabs, files, notes, search, docs, and workflows become part of one operating system instead of isolated tools.

Does it actually remember me?

That is the point. The system is built around durable memory, searchable context, saved facts, and reusable skills so you stop re-explaining the same preferences and projects.

What makes this different from a normal chatbot?

A normal chatbot gives you answers. This gives you a managed agent system with memory, tools, practical workflows, and lessons so you can get to real use instead of chasing prompts, models, and settings.

What if I have already tried agent demos?

That is exactly the gap we care about. Most people do not need another flashy demo. They need help turning what they saw into repeatable personal workflows and real business operations.

Do I need to manage models, tokens, or tool updates?

No. MindVault Studio keeps watch on the agentic tool landscape every day. We handle token usage, model selection, tool changes, and useful new features so you can stay focused on outcomes instead of AI plumbing.

What is Agent Quest?

Agent Quest is the guided launch path inside MindVault Personal. You complete practical missions, build agents, earn XP, keep streaks, and unlock templates or tool recipes as your system gets more useful.

Who is learn.trustmvs.com for?

Founders, creators, operators, curious professionals, and anyone who wants a personal AI system that is useful in real life, not just fun for five minutes.
